Privacy Policy – PASS Password Manager

PASS is a privacy-first, offline-based password and credential manager. Your privacy, data ownership, and control are the core principles of this app. We do not collect, store, track, transmit, or analyze any personal or credential data.

1. Data Collection Policy

PASS does not collect any data. There is no registration, no login system, no user account, and no backend server.

We do not request your name, email, phone number, device data, IP address, usage analytics, cookies, or any form of personal identification.

2. How Your Data Is Stored

All credentials you add inside PASS are stored locally in your own browser using browser storage. This data remains on your device only.

Your passwords, usernames, bank details, API keys, or any custom fields never leave your device unless you manually export them.

3. No Cloud, No Server, No Database

PASS does not use cloud storage, external databases, or remote servers. We do not sync data online and we do not have access to your information.

Even we, as developers, cannot see or recover your data. Everything is fully under your control.

4. How You Use PASS

Using PASS is simple and fully manual:

  • Open the PASS web app
  • Add the app or service you want to store credentials for
  • Enter and update your credentials as needed
  • Your data stays saved locally until you delete or update it

5. Import & Export Functionality

PASS provides an import and export system to help you protect your data during browser cache clearing, history deletion, or device changes.

Export

When you export, all saved credentials are downloaded as a structured JSON file. This file is generated only on your device.

Export is recommended before:

  • Clearing browser cache or data
  • Resetting your browser
  • Changing devices

Import

After clearing browser data or reinstalling the app, you can import the previously exported JSON file to restore all credentials.

Once imported successfully, you may safely delete the exported file or store it securely offline as a backup.

6. Full User Control

You have complete control over your data at all times.

  • You decide what to save
  • You decide when to update or delete credentials
  • You decide when to export or import data

7. User Responsibility

Because PASS does not use cloud recovery, users are responsible for:

  • Keeping their device secure
  • Exporting data before clearing browser storage
  • Protecting exported files from unauthorized access

8. Third-Party Access

PASS does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising networks, trackers, or social media platforms.

No third party can access your credentials through PASS.

9. Privacy Policy Updates

If the privacy policy is updated, changes will be reflected directly within the app. Continued use of PASS means you accept the updated policy.

10. Summary

PASS is designed for users who want maximum privacy, full ownership, and zero dependency on cloud services.

Your data stays in your hands — always.
